FAQ

How do I browse on Book Boutique Store?

All pages of Book Boutique are organized to provide you with various ways of browsing the entire site. In each page you can find on navbar and on footer the link to the main page.

Where can I find the book?

If you are looking for a particular product, enter the keyword that best describes it in the search engine hole (on top, in the navbar). Otherwise if you are on the home page click on the categories drop-down menu and after press the category you were looking for.

Where can I find the information about product?

Each product is accompanied by an information sheet with all the personal and commercial details that interest you. Here you will also find information on the availability of the product.

Where can I find the information about shipping?

All information about shipping is available here.

How can I buy the products that interest me?

  1. first you need to register on the website and log in.
  2. Whenever you find a product that interests you, click the button "add to chart".
  3. When you have chosen everything you wish to purchase go to the "shop" page, to review the list of products included and the total price. From here you can change the quantity, remove those present, and then proceed with the purchase.

Payment Methods

Buyers can pay with all major credit cards or PayPal. The credit cards we accept are:

  • Visa
  • MasterCard
  • American Express
  • JCB
  • Union Pay

We do not accept:

  • Bank notes
  • International money orders
  • Paypal account
  • SKRILL account
  • Bank transfers
  • Payment by installments

Shipping and Returns

Delivery Mode

Book Boutique ships in Italy. The shipping methods you can choose are:

  • Fast Delivery:

    Daily shipping by express courier. Delivery within 1-2 working days from product retrieval

  • Standard Delivery:

    Daily shipping by express courier Delivery within 3-5 working days from product retrieval

Shipping Time

The time it takes to deliver your purchases to your home results from this sum: time of preparation of the order for shipping, plus, delivery time by the chosen carrier. The first depends on the availability of the product and refers to the time required to find the products from our suppliers and prepare the package, to then proceed with the shipment. The second depends on the carrier and it is the time it takes the courier to actually deliver the package to your address.

Order Cancellation

It is possible to cancel an order until it is still being prepared. You can follow and act on your order on the "My orders" page. if an order has already been prepared and sent, it cannot be canceled

Tracking

You cannot keep track of your orders for now. The functionality is almost complete and will be available soon
